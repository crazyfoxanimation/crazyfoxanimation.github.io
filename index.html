About Me

I’m Andy Smith. I’ve been lucky enough to spend over two decades in the 3D animation world, helping clients take a rough idea and transform it into something they’re proud to show off.

My journey has taken me across the globe, from the UK to Australia, working on diverse projects spanning TV programming, hologram content, and digital media.

My process is deeply rooted in concept development. I love the "spark" of a new project and excel at taking an initial idea and quickly evolving it into a polished, tangible solution that hits your goals. I’m just as comfortable pitching the vision as I am at the workstation executing the final frames.

Beyond 3D animation, I’m also passionate about helping clients navigate the future of visual communication through immersive Virtual Reality experiences and 360 Virtual Tours.

Available for freelance contracts, creative collaboration, or long-term roles.
